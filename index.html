About Sri Mallikarjuna Temple

A Sacred Place of Mythological Significance

Mrugavadhe is a place with limited awareness yet of historical and mythological significance. According to the Indian Hindu scripture "Ramayana", this place is where the demon Maricha, who came in the form of a golden deer to take away Lord Rama from Sita, was killed.

After slaying the demon, Rama installed a Shivalinga here. This temple, originally named Malahanikareshwar, is today known as Mallikarjuna Temple. The temple features a special 4.5 feet tall Shivalinga, making it a unique place of worship.
