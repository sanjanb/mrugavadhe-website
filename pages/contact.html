Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about visiting the temple

What are the temple visiting hours?

The temple is open for darshan from 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM daily. During festivals, the timings may be extended.

Is advance booking required for special pujas?

Yes, we recommend advance booking for special pujas, especially during festivals. You can book through our contact numbers or visit the temple office.

Are there accommodation facilities near the temple?

Basic accommodation facilities are available in Mrugavadhe village. For better facilities, you can stay in Thirthahalli, which is 26 km away.

What is the best time to visit the temple?

Early morning hours (6:00-8:00 AM) are ideal for peaceful darshan. Avoid monsoon season (June-September) due to heavy rainfall in the region.

Can we take photographs inside the temple?

Photography is allowed in the temple premises but not inside the main sanctum. Please respect the temple rules and maintain silence during prayers.
