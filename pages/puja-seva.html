Puja & Seva Booking

Perform Sacred Rituals and Seek Divine Blessings

Available Puja Services

Rudrabhisheka

₹501 - ₹5001

Sacred abhisheka of Lord Mallikarjuna with milk, honey, ghee, and other sacred items while chanting Rudram.

  • Ganesha Puja
  • Rudram Chanting
  • Panchamrita Abhisheka
  • Aarti & Prasada
Duration: 1-2 hours

Sahasranama Archana

₹101 - ₹501

Chanting of 1008 names of Lord Shiva with flowers and bilva leaves offering.

  • Ganesha Puja
  • 1008 Names Chanting
  • Bilva Leaf Offering
  • Prasada
Duration: 45 minutes

Laghu Rudra Homa

₹2501 - ₹11000

Sacred fire ritual with 121 recitations of Rudram for special occasions and wishes.

  • Ganesha & Kalash Puja
  • Rudra Homa (121 times)
  • Purnahuti
  • Prasada & Vibhuti
Duration: 3-4 hours

Panchagavya Abhisheka

₹1001 - ₹2501

Special abhisheka with five sacred cow products for purification and blessings.

  • Milk, Curd, Ghee, Urine & Dung
  • Sacred Mantras
  • Flower Decoration
  • Special Aarti
Duration: 1.5 hours

Maha Mrityunjaya Japa

₹251 - ₹1001

Powerful mantra chanting for health, protection from negativity and spiritual growth.

  • 108/1008 Mrityunjaya Mantras
  • Bilva Leaf Offering
  • Sacred Thread
  • Blessed Prasada
Duration: 30-60 minutes

Kalash Puja

₹151 - ₹501

Sacred pot worship for prosperity, peace and removal of vastu doshas.

  • Ganesha Invocation
  • Kalash Establishment
  • Mantras & Offerings
  • Holy Water (Theertham)
Duration: 30 minutes
