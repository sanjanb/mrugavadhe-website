Festivals & Events

Celebrate Divine Occasions at Our Sacred Temple

Major Annual Festivals

Major Festival
14 Feb 2025

Maha Shivaratri

The Great Night of Lord Shiva

The most sacred night dedicated to Lord Shiva. Special abhisheka, all-night chanting of Shiva mantras, and continuous darshan.

Special Activities:

  • Continuous Rudrabhisheka
  • All-night vigil and prayers
  • Devotional singing and bhajans
  • Free prasada distribution
Duration: Full day & night (6:00 PM - 6:00 AM next day)
Temple Festival
21 Mar 2025

Annual Rathotsava

Grand Chariot Festival

The temple's grandest celebration featuring the sacred chariot procession of Lord Mallikarjuna through the village streets.

Procession Schedule:

  • Morning: Deity preparation and decoration
  • Afternoon: Grand chariot procession
  • Evening: Special homa and aarti
  • Cultural programs and festivities
Duration: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Sacred Day
15 Apr 2025

Ram Navami

Lord Rama's Birthday

Special celebration of Lord Rama's birth, commemorating his connection to this sacred place where he installed the Shivalinga.

Special Observances:

  • Ramayana recitation
  • Special pujas to Lord Rama
  • Abhisheka to Shivalinga
  • Distribution of prasada
Duration: 6:30 AM - 9:00 PM

Monthly & Special Occasions

Pradosh Vrat

Twice Monthly (13th day of lunar fortnight)

Special evening prayers during the auspicious pradosh kaal, ideal for seeking Lord Shiva's blessings.

5:30 PM - 7:30 PM

Ekadashi

Twice Monthly (11th day of lunar cycle)

Sacred day for fasting and prayers, with special abhisheka and extended darshan hours.

6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Somvar (Monday) Special

Every Monday

Mondays are especially sacred to Lord Shiva. Special abhisheka with milk and bilva leaves.

6:00 AM onwards

Karthik Maas

November (Entire Month)

Sacred month with daily deepa puja, special prayers, and extended temple decoration with lights.

Daily Special Programs

Shravan Maas

July-August (Monsoon Month)

Most auspicious month for Lord Shiva worship. Daily abhisheka and special Kanwar Yatra welcomes.

Enhanced Daily Rituals

Ganesh Chaturthi

August/September

Special prayers to Lord Ganesha with elaborate decorations and modak offerings.

6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Festival Participation Guidelines

Arrival Time

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the scheduled time for major festivals to secure a good darshan position.

Offerings

Special offerings for each festival are welcomed. Consult our priests for appropriate items and quantities.

Crowd Management

During major festivals, please follow queue instructions and maintain discipline for everyone's safety and comfort.

Photography

Photography is permitted in designated areas only. Please respect the sanctity during active rituals and prayers.

Parking

Special parking arrangements are made during major festivals. Follow the guidance of our volunteers for parking.

Volunteering

Devotees interested in helping during festivals can contact the temple office to register as volunteers.
