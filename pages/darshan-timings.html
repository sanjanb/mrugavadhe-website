Darshan Timings

Plan Your Sacred Visit

Daily Darshan Schedule

Regular temple opening hours and darshan timings

Morning Darshan

6:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Temple opens with Suprabhatam
  • Morning Abhisheka at 6:30 AM
  • Continuous darshan available
  • Best time for peaceful worship

Evening Darshan

4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Temple reopens with Sandhya Aarti
  • Special prayers at 6:00 PM
  • Evening Abhisheka at 7:00 PM
  • Temple closes with final aarti

Daily Aarti Schedule

Experience the divine atmosphere during these special prayer times

6:30 AM

Suprabhatam & Mangala Aarti

Morning prayers to awaken the Lord with devotional songs

12:00 PM

Madhyahna Aarti

Midday prayers before the temple closes for afternoon rest

6:00 PM

Sandhya Aarti

Evening prayers with traditional oil lamps and incense

8:00 PM

Shayan Aarti

Final prayers before the temple closes for the night

Special Occasions & Festival Timings

Extended darshan hours during festivals and special days

Maha Shivaratri

February 14, 2024

Special Timing: 6:00 AM - 12:00 AM (Next Day)

Night Long Darshan: Continuous darshan and prayers

  • Rudra Abhisheka every 2 hours
  • Special bhajans and kirtans
  • Free prasadam distribution

Mondays (Somvar)

Every Monday

Extended Hours: 5:30 AM - 8:30 PM

Special Features: Extended morning prayers

  • Extra Abhisheka at 5:30 AM
  • Monday special prayers
  • Extended evening aarti

Shravan Month

July - August

Special Timing: 5:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Kanwar Yatra: Special arrangements for pilgrims

  • Extended darshan hours
  • Special Abhisheka ceremonies
  • Prasadam and water facilities

Ram Navami

April 17, 2024

Special Timing: 5:30 AM - 9:30 PM

Ramayana Connection: Special prayers commemorating Lord Rama

  • Special Rama prayers
  • Ramayana recitation
  • Cultural programs

Important Information

Please note these important details for your visit

Temple Closure

The temple remains closed from 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM daily for the afternoon rest of the deity.

Festival Changes

Timings may vary during festivals and special occasions. Please check our announcements.

Best Visit Time

Early morning (6:00-8:00 AM) is ideal for peaceful darshan and photography.

Confirm Timings

During monsoon or special circumstances, please call to confirm temple timings.
